# Sample Prompts for AI Image Generation
# =======================================
# Use these as templates for character and scene generation

## Character Portrait
 cinematic portrait of a character, ultra realistic, soft lighting, 8k resolution, highly detailed, professional photography

## Scene Background
 wide establishing shot, cinematic environment, dramatic lighting, detailed background, atmospheric perspective, golden hour lighting

## Character with Action
 dynamic action pose, motion blur, intense expression, cinematic composition, dramatic shadows, professional color grading

## Consistent Character (for IPAdapter)
 same character, consistent face features, matching clothing, coherent style, maintained proportions, recognizable likeness

## Negative Prompts (what to avoid)
 blurry, low quality, distorted, ugly, deformed, bad anatomy, extra limbs, watermark, signature, cropped, worst quality

## Style Modifiers
 anime style, photorealistic, oil painting, watercolor, digital art, concept art, storybook illustration, cinematic, noir

## Lighting Options
 soft lighting, dramatic lighting, rim lighting, studio lighting, natural lighting, golden hour, blue hour, neon lighting, volumetric lighting
